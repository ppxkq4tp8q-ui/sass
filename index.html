        How this works

        This tool is a structured conversation aid, not a measurement instrument. The maturity stages (Reactive → Emerging → Embedded → Leading) reflect patterns drawn from AI adoption research — they describe what organizations at each stage tend to experience, not a precise calculated score.

        Each lever represents a distinct organizational decision with real consequences. Levers are grouped into three domains: Strategy (vision, portfolio, investment), Governance (policy, risk, accountability), and Execution (operating model, skills). The "where to start" recommendations are generated by identifying which reactive or partial levers carry the most long-term leverage — specifically those whose absence tends to block progress on all the others.

        Use the output as a prompt for honest conversation, not as a verdict. The most valuable thing this tool can do is help your leadership team agree on where you actually are.
