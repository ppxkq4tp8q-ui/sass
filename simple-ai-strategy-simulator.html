How the scoring model works

Each lever is scored on a 0–2 scale (Reactive = 0, Partial = 1, Strategic = 2). Levers are weighted differently across three time horizons — because some decisions pay off quickly (e.g., policy clarity) while others compound over years (e.g., vision alignment, investment). The overall strategic effectiveness score is a weighted average normalized to 100. The "value left on table" bars show the proportional gap between your current setting and the strategic optimum for each lever.
